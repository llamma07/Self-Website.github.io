GLADIOLUS

Written by Ashvik Velagala

Logline:

A dying hitman, seeking to reconnect with his estranged son, takes up a lucrative offer to provide for his son, but finds himself entangled in a deadly drug case as his past catches up to him.



Genres:

Crime Thriller



Format:

Feature



Length:

180 Pages/3 Hours



Summary:

Jake, a seasoned hitman with a troubled past, embarks on a perilous journey to mend his fractured relationship with his estranged son. Faced with the weight of impending death, Jake finds himself at a crossroads. It is then that a chance encounter thrusts him into a high-stakes mission proposed by a group of young individuals.



Meanwhile, Matthew, a conflicted teenager believed to be Andrew's successor is torn between loyalty and a yearning for redemption. Matthew grapples with the burden of guilt, while his steadfast sister, a beacon of hope, stands by his side.


Simultaneously, a pair of vengeful twins, Leo and Flynn, plunge headlong into their own vendetta against Andrew. Driven by a shared tragedy, their relentless pursuit takes them down a treacherous path, one that will eventually intertwine with the fates of Jake, Matthew, and the group of disparate individuals united against a common enemy.


As the intricate threads of their stories begin to unravel, long-held secrets are unearthed, revealing a tapestry of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, the stakes soar higher, pushing the boundaries of trust and loyalty to their breaking points.
