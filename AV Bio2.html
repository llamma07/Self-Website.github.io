About Ashvik Velagala


Hi! My name is Ashvik, and I'm a 16-year-old filmmaker. From a young age, I've had a passion for creating stories and bringing them to life through the medium of film. It all began when I was just a child, and I discovered the magical world of movies. However, what truly captivated me was not just watching films, but understanding the intricate process behind their creation.


As a child, I would often find myself immersed in the world of storytelling. At the tender age of 6, I started crafting my own tales, fueled by my vivid imagination and a desire to share my narratives with others. Initially, my primary audience consisted of my parents, who always lent a patient ear to my imaginative tales. Occasionally, I would also gather my friends around, eager to captivate them with the stories that unfolded in my mind.


It wasn't until I turned 14 that my interest in filmmaking reached its peak. This newfound passion led me to delve deeper into the art of screenwriting, a skill that I quickly developed and honed. I drew inspiration from the works of renowned filmmakers like Christopher Nolan, particularly his mind-bending masterpiece "Inception," and M. Night Shyamalan, whose film "Unbreakable" left a lasting impression on me. These cinematic experiences ignited a fire within me and solidified my determination to pursue a career in the film industry.


However, beyond the influence of these exceptional filmmakers, what truly fuels my passion for writing and filmmaking is the unwavering support I have received from my loved ones. My parents, in particular, have been my pillars of encouragement, always willing to listen to my stories, provide constructive criticism, and offer invaluable guidance. Their belief in my talent and dedication has given me the confidence to pursue my dreams fearlessly.


Moreover, my grandparents have played a significant role in nurturing my creative spirit. They have shared their wisdom and experiences, instilling in me a deep appreciation for storytelling and the power it holds. Their unwavering support has been a constant source of motivation, reminding me to never give up on my aspirations.
